## Planet Archetypes

### ☀ Sun — The Self
The conscious ego, life force, and core identity. Where the Sun falls reveals what the person is here to *become*.

### ☽ Moon — The Soul
Emotions, instincts, and subconscious patterns. The Moon governs how one feels and responds, not just how one thinks.

### ☿ Mercury — The Mind
Language, thought, and perception. Mercury rules how a person processes and communicates reality.

### ♀ Venus — The Heart
Love, beauty, and value. Venus reveals what a person desires and what draws others toward them.

### ♂ Mars — The Will
Action, aggression, and desire. Mars governs the engine of ambition and the nature of conflict.

### ♃ Jupiter — The Benefactor
Luck, growth, and philosophy. Jupiter expands whatever it touches — for better or worse.

### ♄ Saturn — The Teacher
Karma, limitation, and mastery. Saturn demands discipline and rewards patience with enduring achievement.

### ♅ Uranus — The Awakener
Revolution, disruption, and genius. Uranus breaks what no longer serves so something new can emerge.

### ♆ Neptune — The Mystic
Dreams, illusion, and transcendence. Neptune dissolves boundaries between self and the infinite.

### ♇ Pluto — The Transformer
Death, rebirth, and hidden power. Pluto governs the deep undercurrents that reshape a life from within.
