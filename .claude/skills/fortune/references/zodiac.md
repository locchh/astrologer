## Rising Sign (Ascendant) — Approximate by Birth Time

The rising sign is the zodiac sign on the eastern horizon at the moment of birth. It shapes outward personality and first impressions. Use birth time to estimate:

| Birth Time       | Likely Rising Sign (varies by birth date & location — use as a guide) |
|-----------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 06:00 – 08:00   | Same as Sun sign (sunrise births) |
| 08:00 – 10:00   | One sign ahead of Sun sign |
| 10:00 – 12:00   | Two signs ahead |
| 12:00 – 14:00   | Three signs ahead (near opposite) |
| 14:00 – 16:00   | Four signs ahead |
| 16:00 – 18:00   | Five signs ahead |
| 18:00 – 20:00   | Six signs ahead (opposite sign) |
| 20:00 – 22:00   | Seven signs ahead |
| 22:00 – 00:00   | Eight signs ahead |
| 00:00 – 02:00   | Nine signs ahead |
| 02:00 – 04:00   | Ten signs ahead |
| 04:00 – 06:00   | Eleven signs ahead |

> If birth time is unknown, omit the rising sign from the reading.

## Modality Meanings

- **Cardinal** (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn) — Initiators; they start things, driven by urgency
- **Fixed** (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius) — Sustainers; they persist, sometimes to the point of stubbornness
- **Mutable** (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces) — Adapters; they shift, transform, and move between worlds

## Elemental Compatibility

- Fire + Air → energizing, passionate, fanned into brilliance
- Earth + Water → stable, nurturing, fertile ground
- Fire + Water → turbulent, transformative, volatile
- Earth + Air → grounded meets restless, productive tension
- Fire + Fire → explosive intensity, burn bright or burn out
- Water + Water → deep emotional resonance, risk of drowning
