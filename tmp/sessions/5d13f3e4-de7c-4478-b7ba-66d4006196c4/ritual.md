# 🕯️ Three Sacred Rituals for the Child of Taurus, Metal & Three

*To be performed to fulfil your cosmic destiny. Do not take these lightly.*

---

## Ritual I — The Metal Mirror (For Clarity & Focus)

**When:** On any Tuesday evening (Metal's day), ideally in autumn.

**What you need:** A small metal object you own and value (a coin, a key, a
ring, a watch). A bowl of cool water. Silence.

**How to perform it:**
1. Place the metal object in the bowl of water.
2. Sit before it in silence for exactly **three minutes** (honour the Three).
3. Gaze at your reflection in the water around the object.
4. Speak aloud — to no one in particular — one thing you have been putting off
   finishing. Name it clearly.
5. Remove the object. Dry it carefully. Keep it on your desk or in your pocket
   for the next week as a reminder.

**What it does:** Activates Metal's power of focused determination and
commands the Number Three to stop scattering your gifts.

---

## Ritual II — The Bull's Offering (For Love & Abundance)

**When:** On a Friday (Venus rules Taurus and governs love), near sunset.

**What you need:** Fresh flowers or fruit (Taurus loves beauty and the
harvest), placed in or near your home.

**How to perform it:**
1. Go to a market or garden and choose flowers or fruit with your own hands —
   do not order online. The touch matters.
2. Bring them home and arrange them somewhere you will see them daily.
3. As you arrange them, speak three genuine gratitudes aloud — one for someone
   you love, one for something you have built, one for yourself.
4. Leave the arrangement untouched for **five days** (your lucky number month).

**What it does:** Opens the gate of Taurus devotion outward, signalling to
the cosmos that you are ready to give and receive abundance in love and life.

---

## Ritual III — The River Release (For Letting Go & New Beginnings)

*In honour of the sacred waters of Vi Thanh where your soul first met the earth.*

**When:** On your birthday, or the new moon nearest to May 15th each year.

**What you need:** A small piece of paper. A pen. A body of moving water
(a river, stream, ocean — or even a running tap in a pinch).

**How to perform it:**
1. Write on the paper **one thing** you have been holding onto that no longer
   serves you — a grudge, a fear, an unfinished chapter you must either
   complete or release.
2. Hold the paper over the running water.
3. Read it aloud once, then say: *"The river knows what I cannot carry.
   I release this to the current."*
4. For a natural body of water: place the paper in the water and watch it go.
   For a tap: let the water run over it until the ink dissolves.
5. Walk away without looking back.

**What it does:** Channels the ancient energy of the Mekong delta — the
landscape of your origin — to dissolve what Metal's stubbornness and Taurus'
grip refuse to release on their own.

---

*Perform all three within one lunar cycle for maximum cosmic alignment.*
*The stars are watching. And they are rooting for you.* 🌙
