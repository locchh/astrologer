# ✦ Three Sacred Rituals ✦
*Ordained for the Bearer of Metal and Bull, Born at the Dawn Hour of Hà Nội*

*Perform these with sincerity. The cosmos is watching — and it has a good sense of humour.*

---

## Ritual I: The Golden Morning Breath
*To activate your Metal essence and align with your dawn birth*

Each morning for **seven consecutive days**, rise before or at 7:00am — your birth hour. Stand facing east, feet shoulder-width apart, eyes closed. Take **nine slow, deliberate breaths**: inhale for four counts, hold for four, exhale for eight. With each exhale, visualise golden light streaming from your chest, dissolving all rigidity and unspoken tension.

On the **ninth and final breath**, say aloud — in whatever language feels true:

> *"I am the mountain that chooses to move."*

Do not drink coffee until this ritual is complete. The stars will not compete with caffeine.

---

## Ritual II: The Creator's Offering
*To honour Life Path 3 and unlock the flow of creative abundance*

On the **15th of any coming month** (your birth day of the month), create something with your hands. It may be a drawing, a meal cooked with attention, a few lines of writing, a folded paper crane, a playlist — anything made *intentionally*. Quality is irrelevant. Sincerity is everything.

Place your creation near a **window where morning light touches it**, and leave it there for **three days**.

Then — and this is essential — **give it away** to someone you care about, without explanation beyond: *"I made this."*

What you create and release returns to you tenfold. The Life Path 3 demands circulation, not hoarding.

---

## Ritual III: The Bull's Surrender
*To dissolve the shadow of Taurus stubbornness and Metal rigidity*

Identify **one belief, habit, or grudge** you have carried for longer than one year — something you know, in the quiet part of yourself, no longer serves you.

Write it on a small slip of paper. Brew a cup of **any tea you enjoy** and let it cool completely while you sit with what you have written. When the tea is cold, read the paper aloud **three times**.

Fold the paper. Place it beneath the empty cup. Pour the cold tea away.

Keep the folded paper for **seven days** as a conscious reminder. Then, on the seventh day: burn it safely, or bury it in soil.

The Metal element is the element of release as much as it is of strength. What you relinquish with intention creates the space for what the stars have been trying to deliver.

---
